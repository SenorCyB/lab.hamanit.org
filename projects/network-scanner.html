What I Do

Build Process

Every build starts with a conversation. I like understanding how the system will actually be used — what performance matters most, what aesthetic the person wants, and where the budget should flex. From there, I translate that into a practical parts list and build plan.

  • Consult to understand goals, budget, and intended use
  • Choose parts that balance performance, compatibility, and value
  • Assemble with attention to thermals and cable routing
  • Troubleshoot hardware, BIOS, drivers, and stability issues
  • Refine builds to match vision and real-world constraints
